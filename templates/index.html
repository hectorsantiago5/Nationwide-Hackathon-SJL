The Social Justice League

Our Mission

Our mission is to try to understand the correlation between COVID-19 vaccination facilities and underrepresented communities. Even though minority groups are disproportionately affected by COVID-19, in many places testing sites are primarily located in less diverse neighborhoods. This can be due to discrimination, educational, income, and wealth gaps.

The lack of testing sites cause many minority groups to travel miles to be tested, or just not be tested at all. Our goal is to make the connection between the proximity of sites to these communities and the effects it has on them.
